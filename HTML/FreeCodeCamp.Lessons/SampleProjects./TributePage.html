Dr. Jose Rizal

The Filipino polymath and a national hero

A time line on Dr. Jose Rizal's life:

  • June 1861 - Jose Rizal was born in Calamba, Laguna
  • June 1869 - Rizal leaves his hometown Calamba to study in Binan.
  • December 1870 - Rizal returns to Calamba after receiving a letter from her sister Saturnina.
  • June 1876 - Rizal receives a Bachelor's degree from Ateneo De Manila University, graduating with highest honors.
  • June 1877 - In June, Rizal begins to study philosophy at Sto. Tomas University in Manila
  • November 1877 - Rizal writes a poem and receives recognition from his writing from the Royal Economic Society of Friends of the Country, also known as Amigos del Pals. The recognition comes in the form of a diploma of merit and honorable mention.
  • 1882 - Rizal travels secretly from Manila to Spain.
  • 1886 - Rizal receives a degree in medicine from the Central Univesity of Madrid at the age of 23. He becomes an assistant to Dr. L.de Wecker and visited many univesities in Berlin, Leipzig, and Heidelberg in Germany.
  • 1887 - Rizal finishes his first novel titled Noli Me Tangere while staying in Berlin.
  • September 1891 - The first copies of El Filibusterismo was released.
  • 1892 - Rizal was arrested after anti-friar bills were found inside the baggage of Lucia, his sister. He was imprisoned at Fort Santiago and transferred to Dapitan after nine days.
  • 1896 - His request to go to Cuba was approved. On the way to Cuba, he was arrested and charged with conspiracy and sedition.
  • December 30 1896 - Rizal was executed by firing squad.
